Bio

Background

I am a Biomedical and Mechanical Engineering student at the University of Ottawa, graduating in April 2028. My work spans biomedical device design, mechanical system prototyping, and safety-critical engineering projects in defense and medical applications.

I have hands-on experience with SolidWorks, 3D printing, mechanical design, embedded systems, and cross-disciplinary collaboration in student-led and co-op projects, including leadership roles in the University of Ottawa Bionics Team and Chem-E-Car Club.

I am passionate about developing technologies that have a direct, positive impact — whether in wearable rehabilitation devices, medical instrumentation, or safety-critical systems in the defense sector. I approach every project with rigorous analysis, iterative design, and a commitment to functional, real-world solutions.
